ECS is a 45’ intermodal logistics operator, specializing in transport between the UK & Ireland and mainland Europe. All their activities are centralized at their head office in Zeebrugge. This well-considered strategy increases noticeably their level of control and efficiency. ECS has offices in Venlo (Netherlands), Le Havre (France), Waterford (Ireland) and Novara (Italy). This geographical decentralization emphasizes their local market-oriented approach.
