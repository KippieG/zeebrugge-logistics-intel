Terminal Services Rail Connection Three times a week, there is a direct connection between Zeebrugge and Antwerp for containers in both directions. Barge connection There is a daily inland shipping connection between Zeebrugge and Antwerp with a transit time of 6 hours. Working at PSA Zeebrugge Do you have a passion for the port and want to apply your expertise in an international, dynamic environment? At PSA Zeebrugge, you contribute to handling and transporting forest and paper products for some of the world's largest paper producers, connecting local and international flows by train, truck, barge, and RoRo ships.
