Our terminals are strategically located across Europe, enabling seamless RoRo logistics – from receiving and storing vehicles to loading and unloading, and prompt release of units for delivery. We understand that efficient and adaptable terminal management plays a vital role in the automotive and breakbulk logistics chain. Our operations can be fully customized to meet individual customer requirements, ensuring flexibility, speed, and precision at every stage. Vehicle Terminal Operations Across Europe We operate specialized vehicle import and export terminals at several key European ports, including Pasajes and Vigo (Spain) and Zeebrugge (Belgium) — managed directly by our teams or in partnership with trusted service providers. Most of our terminals incorporate road and rail connections linked directly to port operations, allowing for efficient multimodal transfers and reduced handling times. Value-added vehicle services Beyond vehicle handling and storage, we offer a comprehensive range of value-added services to enhance the efficiency of your vehicle logistics chain: Pre-delivery inspection (PDI) Vehicle de-waxing and cleaning Minor repairs and paintwork Wrap-guard fitting or removal Accessory installation Body building and technical workshop facilities These integrated services ensure that every vehicle is processed, prepared, and ready for distribution to its final destination. Advanced technology & real-time tracking Since introducing our track-and-trace system, we have consistently invested in cutting-edge terminal management technology. Our systems provide: Real-time visibility of every vehicle movement Web-based tracking applications for easy customer access Full integration with OEM and customer IT systems Our Vehicle Distribution and Terminal Management System (VDTMS) supports critical processes including vehicle receipt, validation, data transmission, inventory management, and damage reporting. By integrating with all major OEM platforms, we ensure accurate communication, transparent reporting, and optimized operational efficiency across every terminal. Your trusted partner in port & terminal logistics Whether you need automotive terminal operations, RoRo port handling, or customized vehicle logistics, UECC offers proven expertise, modern infrastructure, and advanced digital solutions to ensure your supply chain runs smoothly and efficiently.
